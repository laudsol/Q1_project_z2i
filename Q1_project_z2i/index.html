Save the 1% (for yourself)

Having a well-balanced financial portfolio is a key component to planning for your future. Wealth advisors charge between 1% and 2% of assets to structure and monitor your portfolio. Zero-2-invest is a free platform offering easy-to-use portfolio tools, and is dedicated to putting money back where it belongs: in your pocket.


Choose a profile below to get started:

I prefer less risk

Holding lots of low-risk debt (bonds) and some equity (stocks) in mature companies in stable markets will reduce the volatility of your portfolio, making large losses less likely. This allocation is focused on income generation, usually has limited upside, and is designed for capital preservation.

I'm okay with moderate amounts of risk

Investing in a well-balanced portfolio over a long period of time is the best way to grow your assets safely. Exposure mostly consists of a wide range of companies in developed market, as well as some low-to-moderate risk debt.

I can tolerate higher levels of risk

Higher levels of risk can lead to substantially increased performance when managed properly. Increased exposure to small, unintrenched companies, high-risk debt, and emerging and frontier markets will increase volatility over the short term, but also has the potential for greater upside.


Build my own portfolio

Doing deep research on a sector or market you are knowledgeable about can be a rewarding and profitable way to invest. A good rule of thumb is to keep most of your assets in a diversified portfolio, while setting aside 5-10% to 'play' with in the markets.
